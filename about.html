About Us

Welcome to Avanthi's Pet Haven, a trusted place where pets are rescued, cared for, and connected with loving families.

We are passionate about giving homeless and abandoned animals a second chance at life through proper care, protection, and adoption support.

Our mission is to create a safe and happy environment where every pet receives love, medical care, and the opportunity to find a forever home.

At Avanthi's Pet Haven, we believe every animal deserves kindness, compassion, and a family that truly cares.
